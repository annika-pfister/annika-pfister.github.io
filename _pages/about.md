I am a Electrical & Computer Engineering PhD student in the Ingraham Lab at the University of Washington, Seattle. My research interests include rehabilitation robotics, user preference, and user-collaborative engineering. My current research is focused in transcutaneous spinal cord stimulation and exoskeleton walking for adults with spinal cord injury.
