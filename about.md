I'm an electrical engineering PhD student in the Ingraham Lab at the University of Washington, Seattle. My academic interests include closed-loop control for assistive robotics, user preference, and noninvasive neural interfaces. My current research involves transcutaneous spinal cord stimulation and exoskeleton walking for adults with spinal cord injury.
